Usage Logger 2 - Decrypt & Convert

Instructions

To decrypt and convert the data you have collected with Usage Logger 2, click on "Select PDF". After you have selected a PDF file you will be asked to enter a password, supplied by the user. After submitting the password, the PDF is decrypted and converted to a CSV file, which will automatically download. The CSV file will have the same name as the PDF you selected.

Please note, no data is uploaded to the server. This script works locally on your device.
